About US

The Wakiso Miraa Growers and Dealers Association is an umbrella body representing farmers and dealers of miraa (also known as khat) in Uganda.Miraa, also known as khat, holds cultural importance in Uganda as it is used in traditional ceremonies, social bonding, and even spiritual practices.

Key Roles and Strengths

The Wakiso Miraa and growers Association Limited Plays a vital role in suppoerting miraa farmers and traders in uganda.Below are some of key roles and Strengths and they are divided into six sections.

Advocacy

The association advocates for the interests of miraa farmers and traders pushing for policies that benefit the industry.

Market Access

They help farmers acess local and international markets, enabling them to sell their produce at competitive prices.

Training and Capacity Building

They provide capacity building programs for farmers, focusing on best practices,quality control and market requirements.

Regulatory Compliance

They ensure that farmers comply with relevant regulations such as those related to quality control,packaging and export needs.

Research and Development

The Association may engage in research and development activities to improve miraa production,processing and marketing.

Networking and Collaboration

They facilitate networking and collaboration among farmers, traders and other stakeholders for better produce.
